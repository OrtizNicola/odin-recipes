Clams soup

What is Clams soup !?!?!?

It's the dish with the best flavor-difficulty ratio I know. Completely recommended if you are lazy but have a top-tier pristine palate.

Ingredients:

  • 1 1/2 cups of water
  • 1 1/2- inch 3 cm piece fresh ginger, peeled and cut into thin matchstick strips
  • 1 lb. 400 g Manila clams, rinsed and scrubbed
  • 1 tablespoon Chinese rice wine
  • Salt to taste
  • Dash of white pepper

Steps:

  1. Bring the water to boil

  2. Add the ginger and clams. Cover the pot and let cook for about 3-5 minutes, or until all clams are open

  3. Add the Chinese rice wine, salt to taste, and pepper.

  4. Dish out and serve immediately.
